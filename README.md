üìà Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights four crucial metrics to provide an immediate summary of business health:

  * **Total Sales Amount:** The total revenue generated from all sales.
  * **Total Quantity Sold:** The total number of individual items sold.
  * **Total Profit:** The net profit calculated from the sales.
  * **Average Order Value (AOV):** The average amount spent by a customer in a single transaction.
