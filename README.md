üìà Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard highlights four crucial metrics to provide an immediate summary of business health:

  * **Total Sales Amount:** The total revenue generated from all sales.
  * **Total Quantity Sold:** The total number of individual items sold.
  * **Total Profit:** The net profit calculated from the sales.
  * **Average Order Value (AOV):** The average amount spent by a customer in a single transaction.

## üé® Key Visualizations & Analyses

The dashboard is composed of several detailed charts to answer specific business questions:

 1\. Profit or Loss by Month

This chart tracks the store's profitability on a monthly basis, helping to identify seasonal trends or the impact of marketing campaigns.

  * **Chart Type:** Stacked Column Chart
  * **Analysis:** Shows the month-wise contribution of each product to the overall profit or loss.

### 2\. Top 5 Product Categories

Identifies the most significant product categories that are driving the business.

  * **Chart Type:** Horizontal Bar Chart
  * **Analysis:** Ranks product categories based on their performance (e.g., sales amount or profit).

### 3\. Quantity by Category

Provides a clear breakdown of the sales volume for each product category.

  * **Chart Type:** Donut Chart
  * **Analysis:** Shows the percentage of total items sold, broken down by category.

### 4\. Payment Mode Percentage

This visual helps in understanding customer payment preferences.

  * **Chart Type:** Donut Chart
  * **Analysis:** Displays the percentage distribution of transactions across all available payment methods (e.g., COD, Credit Card, UPI).

### 5\. Top 3 States by Amount

Pinpoints the top-performing geographical regions by sales revenue.

  * **Chart Type:** Bar Chart (or Map)
  * **Analysis:** Essential for optimizing regional marketing strategies and logistics.

### 6\. Top 5 Customers

Highlights the most valuable customers based on their total purchase amount.

  * **Chart Type:** Bar Chart
  * **Analysis:** Useful for identifying high-value customers for loyalty programs or targeted marketing.

## üîç Interactive Filter Panel

To make the dashboard dynamic and user-friendly, an interactive filter panel is included.

  * **Filter by Quarter:** Allows the user to slice all dashboard data by Q1, Q2, Q3, or Q4 to analyze performance over specific periods.
  * **Filter by State:** Enables a deep dive into the performance of specific regional markets.
